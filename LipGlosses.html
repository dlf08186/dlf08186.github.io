Lip Gloss Catalog

Each lip gloss is made with vegan and organic materials to make sure your lips get the best look and care. The Vegan Lip Base is made of 100% natural oils and is gluten free. The organic base oil gives the glosses their conditioning and moisturizing properties. The strengthening oils trap the moisture to keep your lips healthy, soft, and shining throughout the day. There are twenty-one color bases to choose from, nine of which are glitter, as well as additional items that can be added for a small fee. There are five tubing selections that are currently available: Golden Wand Tube (10 ml), Squeeze Tube (10 ml or 20 ml), and Lollipop Tube (6 ml or 11 ml).
